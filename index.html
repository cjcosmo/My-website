Bio

Audio Engnieer based out of Southern California

Welcome!

My name is Casey. With two decades of expertise in sound editing and twelve years dedicated to the intricacies of sound mixing for television and film, I have honed my skills to perfection. Recently, I've delved into the world of audio programming, writing Plugins, and expanding my skill set to include video game development.

My journey into the realm of audio began in radio in the early 2000s at WJBD AM/FM in Salem, Illinois. Over the years, I've sculpted my career with a strong work ethic and commitment to delivering audio excellence. In 2011, I proudly graduated with a Bachelor of Science degree in Media Studies from the University of Missouri-St. Louis, marking a pivotal moment that shifted my focus to the world of television and Film.

Over the years, I've lent my expertise to renowned names like Disney, LucasFilm, Marvel, ESPN, Nickelodeon, and the NFL. In addition to my work with major studios and networks, I've had the privilege of collaborating with amazing independent filmmakers. These collaborations have been a source of inspiration, allowing me to bring a diverse range of stories to life through the power of sound.

I invite you to check out some of my work below, to see what I can do to bring your project to life.
